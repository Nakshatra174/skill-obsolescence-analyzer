Skill Obsolescence Analysis System

A data-driven decision support system to evaluate the future relevance of skills in a rapidly evolving job market.

This system helps students and professionals assess the sustainability of technical and non-technical skills based on industry demand, automation risk, and usage trends.

By analyzing historical and domain-specific data, the system provides meaningful insights for career planning and skill development.

Designed as a modular and extensible platform, this application supports future integration with AI models and real-time market intelligence.
